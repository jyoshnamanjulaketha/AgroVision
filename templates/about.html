About Agro Vision

Agro Vision is a comprehensive platform designed to assist farmers and agricultural professionals in making informed decisions. Our tools include crop recommendation systems, crop yield prediction models, and a disease prediction system with traditional steps for mitigation.

We aim to provide practical, easy-to-use tools for anyone in the agricultural field, from small-scale farmers to large agricultural enterprises. The platform integrates machine learning models to optimize recommendations and enhance productivity.

Home Page Overview

The Agro Vision home page provides easy navigation to essential features such as crop recommendation, yield prediction, and disease prediction. The interface is intuitive, allowing users to quickly access the tools they need to improve their agricultural practices.

Crop Recommendation

Our crop recommendation tool analyzes various parameters such as soil type, weather conditions, and historical data to suggest the best crops to grow. It helps farmers make informed decisions to maximize yield and profitability while considering environmental sustainability.

Yield Prediction

The yield prediction feature uses machine learning models to estimate potential crop yield based on factors like climate, soil health, and agricultural practices. This tool helps farmers plan their resources efficiently and optimize crop management for better outcomes.
